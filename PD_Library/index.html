Welcome to Public Domain Library

Public Domain Library is an online source for ebooks that are in the public domain, completely free of charge and without any ads or sketchy downloads.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you find a book??

- You just click on the directory, look through our list of available titles, and select a book. From there, you'll be redirected to a page with key details and short synopsis of the book, in addition to a link to the book in question.

Is this legal?

- Yes, all books in this site are in the public domain, meaning that copyright does not apply to them.

Where does Public Domain Library get the books from?

-All of our books are sourced from Project Gutenburg, a massive online repository of ebooks.
