Hamlet

Author: William Shakespeare

Year Published: Circa 1599-1601

Genre: Tragedy

The king of Denmark is dead, and his brother Claudius sits on the throne- but the dead king's son, Hamlet, can't shake the feeling that something isn't right. After witnessing his dead father's ghost, telling him that Claudius is to blame for his murder, Hamlet begins a quest to find the truth and get his revenge; a quest that will ultimately end in death and destruction for everyone involved.
