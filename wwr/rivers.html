Our Rivers

We proudly offer rafting adventures all across the state of Idaho, and we mainly operate on two rivers: the Snake River and the Salmon River.

Snake River

Our most popular rafting site is the Snake River, where rafters can experience the adventure of one of Idaho's greatest natural features.

Salmon River

If you're looking for something a little wilder, check out the Salmon River! Raft through the rugged wilderness in the majestic Salmon River.
